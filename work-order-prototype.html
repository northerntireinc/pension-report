Create Work Order

Begin a new work order. Enter the customer and add line items the same way you would on a QuickBooks invoice — product/service, description, quantity, and rate. Save it as open; you'll invoice it in step 3.

Open Work Orders

Step 3 · Finish

Create Invoice

Final step. Review the work order, then push it to QuickBooks Online. This creates the invoice in QBO, stamps the invoice number back onto the work order, and marks it invoiced.
