Privacy Policy

Northern Tire — Operating Engineers Local 324 Fringe Remittance · Last updated June 29, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Northern Tire fringe-benefit remittance web app (the “app”) handles your data. The app produces the Operating Engineers Local 324 Shop Agreement remittance (Form 3022 + Form 326) and a weekly payroll-hours email, and runs entirely in your browser.

What data the app stores

The app stores the information you enter to do its job: employees, worked hours, sick/vacation days, fund rates and premiums, contract terms, saved filings, blank form images, and the email targets you configure.

Where your data is stored

All data is stored in your browser’s localStorage on the device that enters it. It is per-device and per-browser — it does not sync between phones or computers, and nothing is sent to any server or third party by the app itself.

What is not collected

The app has no backend, no analytics, no tracking, no cookies for tracking, and no advertising. It does not transmit your data anywhere on its own.

Email features

When you use the one-tap payroll, reminder, or holiday heads-up emails, the app opens your own email client with a pre-filled message addressed to the recipient you configured (for example, your payroll service). Sending the email, and what your email provider does with it, is governed by your email provider’s own terms and privacy policy — not this app.

Form images

Blank union form photos or screenshots you add are stored locally in the browser, the same as all other data. They are not uploaded.

Deleting your data

Clearing your browser’s site data (or using the app’s own clear/reset controls) erases the stored information from that device. Because the data lives only on the device, removing it there removes it completely.

Changes to this policy

If the app gains features that change how data is handled (for example, an optional synced backend), this policy will be updated to reflect that.

Contact

For questions about this policy or the app, contact Northern Tire, Inc.
